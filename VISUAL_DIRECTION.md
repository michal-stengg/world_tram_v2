## Key Traits To Preserve

- Cheerful illustrated world map as the main play surface.
- Chunky tactile UI controls with beveled, toy-like depth.
- Warm, saturated palette with blue skies, vivid oceans, green land, gold trim, and cream panels.
- Friendly captain and train cards with large readable artwork.
- Resource meters across the top for food, fuel, water, and money.
- Large, obvious GO button as the primary turn action.
- Travel-board-game feeling: route tracks, station markers, landmarks, and progress nodes.
- Professional casual game polish rather than rough prototype pixel art.

## Things To Avoid

- Emoji placeholders as final assets.
- Very dark sci-fi or cyberpunk treatment.
- Overly flat vector UI.
- Tiny unreadable text or dense HUD clutter.
- Direct copying of any existing game franchise or character.
